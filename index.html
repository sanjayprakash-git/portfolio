About

I'm a Business Analytics postgraduate with hands-on experience building and scaling ventures from zero. Before stepping into formal analytics, I co-founded Chennai's first chocolate strawberry brand (Happy Dips) — scaling it from a single push cart to 7 franchises — and ran a digital services agency from cold-pitch to delivery.

As a Business Analyst intern, I conducted end-to-end market research for new business concepts, delivering go/no-go recommendations backed by competitive analysis, stakeholder interviews, and financial benchmarking.

I bring a rare combination: the analytical rigour of a trained analyst with the commercial instinct of someone who's actually run a P&L.
